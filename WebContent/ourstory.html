Our Story

"We exist because of the many people around the world who is in search of their own identities and their life purpose"

Mission

Our mission is to help you discover the true you and live with authenticity, aliveness and purpose!

When you know who you are, you feel more authentic and more in tune with yourself.

We work with all kinds of people who feel they have lost a part—or all—of themselves through the ups and downs of life. Struggling to find their true identity, they cannot find the best course to their own true happiness. Through personal guidance, personal training, and real life scenarios, we can help you transform your life so it has direction and meaning that suits you uniquely.

Come discover yourself and discover your true purpose with us at True Life Purpose.

Vision

“Shape the future by restoring people's live, discovering self identities, and sharing our passion with the world.”
